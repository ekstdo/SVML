\[B_{u, v} = \frac{1}{\sqrt{M N}} b_u b_v^T = \frac 1 {\sqrt {MN}} \begin{pmatrix} w^0_M \\ w^u_M \\ w^{2u}_M \\ \vdots \\ w_M^{(M - 1)u} \end{pmatrix} \begin{pmatrix} w_N^0 , w_N^v, \dots, w_N^{(N - 1)v} \end{pmatrix}\] mit $w_N = e^{i \frac{2 \pi} N }$ ZZ: $G, H$ orthogonal $\stackrel{\text{def.}}\Leftrightarrow \langle G, H \rangle = 0$ in das Skalarprodukt eingesetzt: \[\langle B_{u, v}, B_{u\prime, v\prime} \rangle = \sum_{m = 0}^{M - 1} \sum_{n = 0}^{N - 1} \underbrace{\left( \frac 1 {\sqrt{MN}} w^{mu}_M w^{nv}_N\right)^*}_{g_{u, v}^*}\cdot \underbrace{\frac 1 {\sqrt{MN}}w_M^{m u\prime} w_N^{n v\prime}}_{h_{u\prime, v\prime}}\] \[= \frac 1 {MN} \sum_{m = 0}^{M - 1} \sum_{n = 0}^{N - 1} (e^{\frac{2 \pi imu} M} e^{\frac{2 \pi inv} N})^* e^{\frac{2 \pi imu\prime} M} e^{\frac{2 \pi inv\prime} N}\]
\[= \frac 1 {MN} \sum_{m = 0}^{M - 1} \sum_{n = 0}^{N - 1} (e^{\frac{2 \pi imu} M} e^{\frac{2 \pi inv} N})^* e^{\frac{2 \pi imu\prime} M} e^{\frac{2 \pi inv\prime} N}\] \[= \frac 1 {MN} \sum_{m = 0}^{M - 1} \sum_{n = 0}^{N - 1} e^{-\frac{2 \pi imu} M} e^{-\frac{2 \pi inv} N} e^{\frac{2 \pi imu\prime} M} e^{\frac{2 \pi inv\prime} N}\] \[= \frac 1 {MN} \sum_{m = 0}^{M - 1} \sum_{n = 0}^{N - 1} e^{\frac{2 \pi im} M (u\prime - u)} e^{\frac{2 \pi in} N (v\prime - v)}\] \[= \frac 1 {MN} \sum_{m = 0}^{M - 1} e^{2 \pi i\frac{m} M (u\prime - u)} \sum_{n = 0}^{N - 1} e^{2 \pi i\frac{n} N (v\prime - v)}\]
Wir betrachten uns: \[\sum_{n = 0}^{N - 1} e^{2 \pi i\frac{n} N (v\prime - v)}\]
  • $v = v\prime: \sum_{n = 0}^{N - 1} e^{2 \pi i\frac{n} N 0} = N$
  • $v \neq v\prime: \sum_{n = 0}^{N - 1} e^{2 \pi i \frac n N (v\prime - v)}$, geometrische Summe: \[\sum_{k = 0}^{N - 1} q^k = \frac{1 - q^N}{1 - q} \] \[\sum_{n = 0}^{N - 1} (e^{2 \pi i \frac 1 N (v\prime - v)})^n = \frac{1 - (e^{2 \pi i \frac 1 N (v\prime - v)})^N}{1 - e^{2 \pi i \frac 1 N (v\prime - v)}} = \frac{1 - \underbrace{e^{2\pi i (v\prime - v) }}_{= 1}}{1 - e^{2 \pi i \frac 1 N (v\prime - v)}} = 0 \]
